Navigation Styling

XSLT is available for menu formatting, in order to alter naviation markup (for Bootstrap, Foundation, UIKit...)

Simply update the menu XSLT templates found in the template/navigation folder.

Sample TopNav XML

  <root>
    <node title='How It Works' name='HowItWorks' path='/HowItWorks/' />
    <node title='Reference' name='Reference' path='/Reference/' />
    <node title='Work In Progress' name='WorkInProgress' path='/WorkInProgress/' />
    <node title='Tests' name='Tests' path='/Tests/'>
        <node title='Hierarchy' name='Hierarchy' path='/Tests/Hierarchy/'>
            <node title='Child' name='Child' path='/Tests/Hierarchy/Child/'>
                <node title='Grand-child' name='Grand-child' path='/Tests/Hierarchy/Child/Grand-child/' />
            </node>
        </node>
        <node title='Markdown' name='Markdown' path='/Tests/Markdown/' />
        <node title='X M L Template' name='XMLTemplate' path='/Tests/XMLTemplate/' />
        <node title='C S V Template' name='CSVTemplate' path='/Tests/CSVTemplate/' />
        <node title='Calendar' name='Calendar' path='/Tests/Calendar/' />
        <node title='R S S' name='RSS' path='/Tests/RSS/' />
        <node title='Subsitutions' name='Subsitutions' path='/Tests/Subsitutions/' />
        <node title='H T M L' name='HTML' path='/Tests/HTML/' />
    </node>
    <node title='Search' name='Search' path='/Search/' />
    </root>
